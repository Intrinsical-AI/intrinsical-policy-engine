# Boundaries

Intrinsical Policy Engine contains the core engine and an educational starter
pack only.

It does not include:

- regulatory packs;
- real policy calibrations;
- commercial evidence templates;
- sector-specific demos;
- generated real-world outputs;
- private repository history;
- legal advice;
- official guidance;
- certification logic;
- any guarantee of compliance.

The starter pack is illustrative. It exists to show how a pack is structured,
validated, exported, traced, and sealed.
